Why I choose Computer science and engineering as my career

My interest in Computer Science developed essentially because of mathematics and physics. How every problem has a logical and reasonable solution behind it persuaded me to chose a field where I will solve problems using my quantitative skills.
As it is said by one of the great physicist, Galileo Galilei- "The book of nature is written in the language of Mathematics​". I truly believe there is some kind of pattern in each and every part of nature and observing those patterns is one of the fantacies that I have from my childhood.
As I grew older I started appreciating more and more the beautiful order and harmony of programming. Analysing the patterns and developing algorithms are the two key aspects of solving a problem hence I choose Computer Science as my career which act as the sink of all my interests.
Always being in a healthy competitive​ environment around me from the very beginning of my school life acts as a fortune for me. Because of this environment I started taking part in competitive competitions where my main goal is to learn a new concept of problem solving every time. I would like to thank my teachers also who always focused on giving the core concept behind the problem rather than just solving it. Also when I was in my school I used to observe my elder sister who was pursing her Btech in IT. I always get fascinated by watching her engaged in programming and various technical competitions. All these factors sum up together and made me completely sure about taking computer science as my career.
